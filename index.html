Blockbuster Movies of Posters and Trailers

Janatha Garage a 2016 Telugu action-drama, was made on a budget of around ₹50 crore

Janatha Garage, a 2016 Telugu action-drama, was made on a budget of around ₹50 crore and became a major box office success, crossing ₹100 crore worldwide within a week and ultimately grossing over ₹125-150 crore globally, becoming one of the highest-grossing Telugu films of 2016. It had a massive opening, collecting around ₹21 crore in Andhra & Telangana on day one, and emerged as the year's biggest Tollywood opener and a significant hit.

Gabbar Singh budget of ₹30 crore and collected around ₹100-150 crore worldwide

The 2012 Telugu blockbuster Gabbar Singh, starring Pawan Kalyan, had an estimated budget of ₹30 crore and collected around ₹100-150 crore worldwide, becoming one of the highest-grossing films of its time. Directed by Harish Shankar, the action-drama was a massive hit, especially after its 2024 re-release, setting new records for Telugu re-releases in North America and India.

Baahubali The Beginning (2015) and The Conclusion (2017), had a combined budget of approximately ₹430 crore,

The Baahubali franchise, comprising The Beginning (2015) and The Conclusion (2017), had a combined budget of approximately ₹430 crore, with Part 1 costing around ₹180 crore and Part 2 about ₹250 crore, becoming a massive box office success with total worldwide earnings exceeding ₹2,400 crore, solidifying its place as one of India's highest-grossing film series.

Hi Nanna reports suggest it was around ₹40-50 crore

Hi Nanna is a 2023 Telugu romantic drama starring Nani and Mrunal Thakur, directed by Shouryuv, about a single father (Nani) and his young daughter (Kiara Khanna), who has a serious lung condition, as their lives intertwine with a woman (Thakur) connected to their past, exploring themes of love, parenthood, memory, and resilience. The film received positive reviews for its emotional depth, performances, and heartwarming story, becoming a box-office success.
